About

Hi. My name is Ivan, and I am a frontend developer on an exciting path in the world of web technologies. In my 19th year, I am an economics major at MSU, looking to combine knowledge of economic sciences with the unique perspective of frontend development.
My passion for coding led me to participate in the Yandex.Praktikum educational program, where I delve deeper into the world of programming and web application development. Problem solving is not only my professional passion, but also a way to constantly improve myself.
Outside of IT, my life is also packed with activity. Not only do I thrive in the world of code, but also in the gym. I believe that physical activity complements and enhances my creative potential. By the way, I am a Candidate for Master of Sport in streetlifting, first-degree holder in bench press and a second-degree in squats with additional weight. Healthy lifestyle is an important part of my philosophy.
On my life path I appreciate positive people with a sense of humor. I believe that optimism and joy are the keys to successful work and true life satisfaction. If you share my passion for development and live in the rhythm of positivity, I will be glad to meet you and work together!
